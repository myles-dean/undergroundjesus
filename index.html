speakers

Jason Shepperd

Jason Shepperd is the founder and lead pastor of Church Project, a movement of “churches of house churches” that equips believers to rethink modern church models and follow a decentralized, Scripture-rooted expression of Christian community. He also leads the Church Project Network and other initiatives focused on biblical ecclesiology, discipleship, and church multiplication.

Ying
Kai

Ying Kai is a pioneering missionary and co-founder of the global Training for Trainers (T4T) movement, an obedience-based discipleship and church-planting approach that has equipped millions of believers to share the gospel and multiply churches worldwide.

Victor
John

Victor John, an Indian national, served as a pastor for 15 years before shifting to a holistic strategy aiming for a movement among Bhojpuri people. Since the early 1990s he has laboured in planting seed among the Bhojpuri, and continues to play a catalytic role in the large and growing Bhojpuri movement. He is the primary author of Bhojpuri Breakthrough: A Movement that Keeps Multiplying.
